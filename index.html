BIOGRAPHY

FULL STACK WEB DEVELOPER

Enki Ray’s musical odyssey began at 15 with his first guitar. Known for his work with bands like Intertwine, Starter Pack, and Des Sensation, Enki has significantly shaped their unique sounds. As a session guitarist for Save the Youth, Decode, Tears, and others, his versatility shines through. Enki’s career reflects a deep commitment to his craft, blending technical skill with emotional depth and influencing the music scene with every performance. In addition to his musical achievements, Enki Ray has a strong background in programming. With a degree in Information Technology earned in July 2024, he has gained foundational skills in several programming languages, including Java, JavaScript, PHP, Python, and C#. His expertise also includes working with MySQL for database management. Enki’s blend of creative and technical talents highlights his versatility and dedication to both music and technology.
